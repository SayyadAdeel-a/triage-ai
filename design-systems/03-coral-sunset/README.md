# TriageAI Design System 03: Coral Sunset

**Mood:** Warm, energetic, friendly, optimistic

A warm palette with coral as the hero accent. Friendly and lively without being overwhelming. Like a sunset that makes you smile at the end of a long email day.

## Color Palette

| Role | Token | Hex | Description |
|------|-------|-----|-------------|
| Background | `--color-background` | `#FFFAF7` | Warm cream-white |
| Foreground | `--color-foreground` | `#2A1810` | Deep warm brown (near-black) |
| Card | `--color-card` | `#FFFFFF` | Pure white surface |
| Primary | `--color-primary` | `#F97316` | Vibrant coral-orange |
| Primary Foreground | `--color-primary-foreground` | `#FFFFFF` | White text on coral |
| Secondary | `--color-secondary` | `#FFF4ED` | Very light peach tint |
| Muted | `--color-muted` | `#FEF3EE` | Soft apricot background |
| Muted Foreground | `--color-muted-foreground` | `#9A7160` | Warm muted text |
| Accent | `--color-accent` | `#F97316` | Same as primary |
| Destructive | `--color-destructive` | `#EF4444` | Clean red |
| Border | `--color-border` | `#F0E0D4` | Warm sand border |
| Sidebar | `--color-sidebar` | `#FEF3EE` | Soft apricot sidebar |

## Key Design Decisions

- **Larger border radius (0.75rem)** for a softer, friendlier feel
- **Warm undertones everywhere** for approachability
- **Coral as the energy color** for actions and highlights

## Best For

- Users who want email management to feel warm and human
- Creative professionals and small teams
- A cheerful daily tool that doesn't take itself too seriously
