# TriageAI Design System 05: Lemon Drop

**Mood:** Cheerful, energetic, playful, radiant

A bright yellow accent on a clean white canvas. Think the sunniest day of the year captured in an interface. Pure joy, pure energy, zero pretension.

## Color Palette

| Role | Token | Hex | Description |
|------|-------|-----|-------------|
| Background | `--color-background` | `#FFFDF7` | Warm yellow-white |
| Foreground | `--color-foreground` | `#2A2010` | Deep warm brown ink |
| Card | `--color-card` | `#FFFFFF` | Pure white surface |
| Primary | `--color-primary` | `#EAB308` | Vibrant golden yellow |
| Primary Foreground | `--color-primary-foreground` | `#2A2010` | Dark text on yellow (high contrast) |
| Secondary | `--color-secondary` | `#FEF9E7` | Very light cream tint |
| Muted | `--color-muted` | `#FEFCE8` | Soft lemon background |
| Muted Foreground | `--color-muted-foreground` | `#927C4A` | Warm golden-gray text |
| Accent | `--color-accent` | `#EAB308` | Same as primary |
| Destructive | `--color-destructive` | `#EF4444` | Clean red |
| Border | `--color-border` | `#F0E6C8` | Warm honey border |
| Sidebar | `--color-sidebar` | `#FEFCE8` | Soft lemon sidebar |

## Key Design Decisions

- **Dark foreground on primary** (not white) for maximum contrast on yellow
- **Larger border radius (0.875rem)** for a bubbly, playful feel
- **Warm undertones** that feel like sunshine

## Best For

- Users who want email to feel fun, not work
- Startups and creative teams
- Anyone who needs a smile when opening their inbox
