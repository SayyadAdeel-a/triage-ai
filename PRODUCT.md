# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
IT support teams, help desk managers, and operations leads who need to automatically categorize, prioritize, and route support tickets using AI. They're overwhelmed by manual triage and want to focus on resolution, not classification.

## Product Purpose
TriageAI automatically triages incoming support tickets using AI, assigning categories, priority levels, and routing them to the right teams. It reduces manual overhead, speeds up response times, and ensures critical issues get attention first.

## Brand Personality
Confident, precise, calm under pressure. The voice of a senior engineer who's seen it all and knows exactly what to do.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages with fake dashboard screenshots and "revolutionary AI" buzzwords
- Overly cheerful startup aesthetics (bright gradients, cartoon illustrations)
- Enterprise software that looks like it was designed in 2015 (cluttered, gray, jargon-heavy)
- Neon/glowing effects that scream "I used an AI template"

## Design Principles
1. Show, don't tell - demonstrate the product's value through clear examples, not empty promises
2. Calm confidence - the interface should feel like a reliable tool, not a toy
3. Precision matters - every word, every pixel should feel intentional
4. Respect the user's time - get to the point, make navigation effortless
5. Professional without being stuffy - approachable but not casual

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Keyboard navigable
- Screen reader compatible
- Reduced motion support
- High contrast ratios on dark backgrounds
